How to Use

Follow these simple steps to install Microsoft Office

1

Download Files

Get the installer files from above and place them in the same folder.

2

Double-click Install-Office-SAFE.bat and grant administrator permission.

3

The installer will guide you through simple questions about what you want to install.

4

Confirm Choices

Review your selections before the download begins.

5

Office will download and install automatically (10-30 minutes).
